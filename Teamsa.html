Highly Experienced

Doctors

Dr Anya Wijesinghe

Companion Animal Veterinarian

BVSc (SL)

Dr Anya Wijesinghe graduated from the University of Peradeniya and joined Petvet Clinic as a trainee Veterinarian soon after. Then, she completed her internship training at Ceylon Grain Elevators.Her special interests include Small Animal Medicine, Veterinary diagnostic imaging and soft tissue surgery.

Dr Janaki Collure

Co-Founder & Director

BVSc.(SL), MSLCVS

Dr Collure is a co-founder and director of PetVet. She previously worked as an Assistant Lecturer in Pharmacology at the Veterinary Teaching Hospital of University of Peradeniya. Dr Collure has trained in clinical practice in the US, the UK, India and Thailand and plays a crucial role in ensuring high standards of care at PetVet.

Dr Nuwan Laknath

Companion Animal Veterinarian

BVSC (Hons) SL, MSc(SL)

Dr Laknath joined the Faculty of Veterinary Science as a Research Assistant to the Wildlife Human Health Net project by the International Development Research Centre-Canada, and Sri Lanka Wildlife Health Center.His special interests in veterinary medicine are surgery, diagnostic imaging and clinical pharmacology.
