Complete Wellness Consultation

As pet owners ourselves, we understand the tangible and intangible benefits of having an animal companion in your life. Bringing a pet inside a home calls for responsible ownership - one that will result in an endlessly positive and joyous relationship for you and your pet

A focus within our Wellness suite of services is to ensure that you, the pet owner, know the value and pleasure of having healthy and happy pets. In order to achieve this, we provide the following consultancy-based services.

  • How to choose a correct pet to fit your lifestyle
  • How to socialize and train
  • How to feed exercise your pet
  • How to correctly manage issues of reproduction
  • How to care for senior animals
